About Me

I grew up in Beaverton, Oregon and came back to the area post-college. I worked in investment consulting for a number of years. It was dreary and lame. I met a friend through my wife who had completed CodeFellows here in PDX, and after a year or so of intermittent discussions, I decided I needed to quit my job and pursue a career as a software developer.

Outside of Alchemy, my wife and I live in Beaverton, and hang out mostly with our cool puppy, Timber. I play golf, hike, do triathlons, and generally enjoy everything outdoors. We love to travel and explore new places, try new things, taste new food, and speak new languages!

I'm not entirely sure what my hopes and aspirations are after Alchemy (beyond the immediate goal of getting a job as a software developer!). I do think it would be enjoyable to work as a dev for serveral years, and perhaps move into product management. Who knows, all I know is I'm excited!
